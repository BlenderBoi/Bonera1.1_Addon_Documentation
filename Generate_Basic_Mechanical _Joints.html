Generate Basic Mechanical Joints

Edit Armature

Pose

Add Simple Limit Rotation Constraint to limit the bone rotation to one axis

Joint Type:

Hinge

Choose to Limit to X or Z Axis of Bone

Pivot / Twist

Choose to Limit to Y Axis of Bone

Hinge Axis: Choose the Axis to Limit to

Hinge Min / Hinge Max: Set the Limit of Minimum and Maximum angle if Limit Angle is On

Limit Angle: Limit the Angle of the Hinge

Affect Transform: Turn on Affect Transform on the Constraint

Lock Location: Lock the Location of the bone

Lock Scale: Lock the Scale of the bone
