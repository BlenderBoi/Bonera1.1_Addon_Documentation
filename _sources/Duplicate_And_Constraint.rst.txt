Duplicate And Constraint
========================

.. list-table::

   * - Object
     - Edit Armature
     - Pose

.. image:: /images/Duplicate_And_Constraint.gif

Duplicate and Constraint will Duplicate the Selected Bones (Edit Armature / Pose Mode) or All Bones (Object Mode)

.. image:: /images/DuplicateAndConstraint.png

**Mode**
   Choose the Method to Rename Your Bone

**Prefix / Suffix / Find and Replace**
   String Used for Renaming

**Position**
   Add the Bone Shape at the Center or 3D Cursor

**Bone Layer**
   Set the Duplicated Bone's Layer

**Deform**
   Make Duplicated Bones Deform Bone / Non Deform Bone
