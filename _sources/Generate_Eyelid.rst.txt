Generate Eyelid
---------------

.. list-table::

   * - Edit Armature

.. image:: /images/Generate_Eyelid.gif

Generate A Eyelid Setup, Select all the Eyelid Bone, and Eyeball Bone Last (Active)

.. image:: /images/Generate_Eyelid.png

**Influence:** The Influence Strength of Eyelid Get Affected by the Rotation of Eyeball

**Parent Mode:** 

    **Eye Parent:** Parent the Eyelid Bone to Eyeball's Parent
    
    **Bone:** Parent the Eyelid Bone to Picked Bone

        **Parent Bone:** Bone Picker for Bone Parent Mode


**Names And Layers Settings:** Set Individual Generated Bone's Prefix and Bone Layers
