.. image:: /images/Generate_Twist_Bone.gif

Generate A Simple Twist Bone Setup

.. admonition:: Note

    This Operators Require the Selected Bones to have at least one child, if multiple child exist, it will use the first child. 

.. image:: /images/Generate_Twist_Bone.png

**Subdivide:** Amount of Cuts to the Bone

**Twist Control Layer:** Bone Layer for Twist Control Bone

**Deform Bone Layer:** Bone Layer for generated twist deformation bones

.. admonition:: Note

    The Selected Bone "Use Deform" will be turn off, while the generated "Twist Bone" will have "Use Deform Turned On Instead"
