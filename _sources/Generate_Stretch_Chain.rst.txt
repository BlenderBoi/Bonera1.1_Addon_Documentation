Generate Stretch Chain
----------------------

.. list-table::

   * - Edit Armature
     - Pose

.. image:: /images/Generate_Stretch_Chain.gif

Make Bone Stretch to other selected bone that have it's head with same location with it's tail

.. admonition:: Note

    It will Do it to Selected Bones, This Works by checking the Bone Head Location instead of checking parenting

.. image:: /images/Generate_Stretch_Chain.png



**Target Bone**
   Prefix Use for the Created Target Bones

**Target Bone Layer**
   Bone Layer for Created Target Bones

**Pre Clear Constraint**
   Clear All Constraint from Selected Bone Before Operation

**Tail Offset**
   Position of Tail Offset from Head
